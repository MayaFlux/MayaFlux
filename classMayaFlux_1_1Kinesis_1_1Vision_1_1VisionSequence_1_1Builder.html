Detailed Description

Fluent builder for VisionSequence.

Each method appends one step and returns *this for chaining. Call build() to produce the final VisionSequence.

auto seq = VisionSequence::Builder{}
.rgba_to_gray()
.gaussian_blur(1.5f)
.threshold(0.4f)
.build();
