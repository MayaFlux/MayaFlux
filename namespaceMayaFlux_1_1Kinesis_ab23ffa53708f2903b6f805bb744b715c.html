Generate a subdivided flat grid in the XZ plane.

Vertices lie at Y = 0. U maps to X, V maps to Z. UV covers [0,1] across the full extent. Normals point along +Y throughout.

Parameters
centerWorld-space centre of the grid.
extent_xTotal width along X.
extent_zTotal depth along Z.
colsNumber of columns (cells along X). Clamped to minimum 1.
rowsNumber of rows (cells along Z). Clamped to minimum 1.
Returns
MeshData ready for TRIANGLE_LIST draw.
