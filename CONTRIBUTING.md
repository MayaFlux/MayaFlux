# Contributor Guidelines

We welcome contributions to this project!  
By participating, you agree to abide by the GPLv3 license **and the following ethical guidelines:**

---

## Ethical Contribution Terms

1. **No Institutional Ownership:**
   Contributions to this project must remain open-source, unencumbered by proprietary claims, employer contracts, or institutional policies that seek to restrict access.

2. **No Curriculum Cooptation:**
   Contributions may not be used to forcibly integrate this work into private, institutional, or commercial teaching frameworks without preserving its open nature and proper credit to original contributors.

3. **Respect Public Benefit:**
   Contributions should align with the project's mission of public, equitable access to knowledge and tools.

---

## How to Contribute

- Fork the repo.
- Make your changes.
- Submit a pull request with a clear description.

All contributions must include:

- Clear documentation.
- Compliance with the licensing terms.

---

## Disclaimer

By contributing, you affirm that your employer or institution has no exclusive claims over your contributions **unless explicitly waived** to maintain GPLv3 compliance.
