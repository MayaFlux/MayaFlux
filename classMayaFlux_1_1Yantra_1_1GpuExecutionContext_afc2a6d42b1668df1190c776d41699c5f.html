Flatten planar double channels to a float staging buffer.

Skipped entirely for structured modalities (glm::vec3 etc.) since those are handled per-binding in prepare_gpu_inputs via PASSTHROUGH or UINT32/INT32 paths — there is no meaningful double representation to flatten from.
