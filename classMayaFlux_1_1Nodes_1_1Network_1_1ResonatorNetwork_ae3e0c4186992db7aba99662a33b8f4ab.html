enum class MayaFlux::Nodes::Network::ResonatorNetwork::FormantPreset : uint8_t
strong

Common vowel and spectral formant configurations.

Provides a starting point for formant synthesis. Frequencies are approximate averages for a neutral adult voice; Q values model typical bandwidths (narrower for higher formants).

Enumerator
NONE 

No preset — all resonators initialised at 440 Hz, Q = 10.
