# Code of Conduct

This project is dedicated to fostering a welcoming, safe, and open environment.

We explicitly prohibit:

- Gatekeeping, coercion, or harassment of contributors or users.
- Institutional appropriation of contributions.
- Retaliation against contributors asserting their ownership and rights.

We stand firmly against the misuse of this project for:

- Institutional moral policing,
- Proprietary restriction,
- Exploitative behavior.

By participating, you agree to uphold these principles.
